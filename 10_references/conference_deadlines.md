## Planning Tips

1. Work backwards from the submission deadline: allow 2-4 weeks for writing, 1-2 months for experiments
2. Most top venues have a rebuttal phase: prepare for reviewer responses
3. Workshop papers have later deadlines and lower acceptance bars - good for early-stage work
4. Challenge participation often leads to workshop papers at MICCAI or ECCV
