# Survey Papers on Image Segmentation

A curated list of comprehensive survey papers covering various aspects of image segmentation.

## General Semantic Segmentation

- **A Survey on Deep Learning Techniques for Image and Video Semantic Segmentation**
  Lateef & Ruichek (2019) | Computers & Graphics
  Overview of deep learning methods for semantic segmentation including FCN, SegNet, DeepLab, PSPNet.

- **Image Segmentation Using Deep Learning: A Survey**
  Minaee et al. (2021) | IEEE TPAMI
  arXiv: [2001.05566](https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.05566)
  Comprehensive survey covering 100+ segmentation architectures across medical, scene, and video domains.

- **A Survey on Semantic Segmentation**
  Hao et al. (2020) | arXiv: [2004.14928](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.14928)
  Overview of semantic segmentation techniques from traditional to deep learning methods.

## Transformer-Based Segmentation

- **A Survey on Vision Transformer**
  Han et al. (2022) | IEEE TPAMI
  arXiv: [2012.12556](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.12556)
  Broad survey on vision transformers covering classification, detection, and segmentation.

- **Transformers in Medical Image Segmentation: A Review**
  Li et al. (2023) | arXiv: [2301.10025](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.10025)
  Focused review of transformer architectures adapted for medical image segmentation.

## Medical Image Segmentation

- **U-Net and its variants for Medical Image Segmentation: A Review of Theory and Applications**
  Siddique et al. (2021) | IEEE Access
  arXiv: [2011.01118](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.01118)
  Comprehensive review of U-Net variants for medical imaging tasks.

- **Medical Image Segmentation: A Review**
  Liu et al. (2021) | arXiv: [2107.00275](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.00275)
  Survey covering CNN-based, transformer-based, and hybrid medical segmentation methods.

- **Deep Learning for Brain MRI Segmentation: State of the Art and Future Directions**
  Akkus et al. (2017) | Journal of Digital Imaging
  Focused survey on deep learning for brain tumor and structure segmentation.

## Instance and Panoptic Segmentation

- **A Survey on Instance Segmentation: State of the art**
  Hafiz & Bhat (2020) | International Journal of Multimedia Information Retrieval
  arXiv: [2007.00047](https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.00047)
  Overview of instance segmentation methods from Mask R-CNN to modern approaches.

- **Panoptic Segmentation: A Review**
  Elharrouss et al. (2021) | arXiv: [2111.10250](https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.10250)
  Survey of panoptic segmentation methods and benchmarks.

## Foundation Models and Prompting

- **A Comprehensive Survey on Segment Anything Model for Vision and Beyond**
  Zhang et al. (2023) | arXiv: [2305.08196](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.08196)
  Extensive survey on SAM architecture, applications, and adaptations.

## Loss Functions

- **A survey of loss functions for semantic segmentation**
  Jadon (2020) | IEEE CIBCB
  arXiv: [2006.14822](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.14822)
  Review of loss functions including cross-entropy, Dice, focal, Lovasz, and their combinations.

## Video Segmentation

- **A Survey on Deep Learning Technique for Video Segmentation**
  Wang et al. (2021) | IEEE TPAMI
  arXiv: [2107.01153](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.01153)
  Survey covering video object segmentation, video semantic segmentation, and video panoptic segmentation.
